About This App

WeatherVue is a web application that provides comprehensive weather information and forecasts.

Designed with a user-friendly interface, the app delivers real-time weather updates, including current conditions, hourly and daily forecasts.

Users can access local weather information as well as forecasts for various locations around the world.

Feature's

Real-Time Weather Updates:

  • Instant access to current weather conditions.

    • Weather Metrics:

  • Temperature: Current and forecasted temperatures.
  • Humidity: Humidity levels for different times of the day.
  • Wind Speed and Direction: Information on wind conditions.

    • Weather with city :

  • Users can access Real-Time weather information for various locations around the world.
